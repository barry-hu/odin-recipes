Fried Rice

Description

This is a recipe for a bowl of delicious fried rice that would include eggs, spam, peas, and scallions

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Eggs
  • Spam
  • Peas
  • Scallions
  • Garlic
  • Salt
  • Sugar
  • Monosodium Glutamate (MSG)
  • Light Soy sauce
  • Sesame Oil
  • Fish sauce
  • Cooking Oil (I prefer grapeseed oil)
  • Chinese cooking wine

Steps

  1. Prep your ingredients
    • Chop up scallions and set aside in a bowl
    • Mince 2 cloves of garlic and set aside
    • Whisk 2 eggs and set aside
    • Cut up spam into cubes (amount to your discretion)
    • Set aside peas
    • Set aside cooked white rice in a bowl
  2. Heat up a wok for a few minutes and then add in oil
  3. First add the eggs in and cook into a scramble then take out and set aside into a bowl
  4. Put in new oil into the empty wok and add rice into the wok
  5. Cook for a few minutes and add in garlic
  6. Toss around until evenly dispersed then add in Spam and peas
  7. Toss around until evenly dispersed then add Light soy, fish sauce, cooking wine, sesame oil
  8. Mix until sauces are evenly distrubted then add in Salt, Sugar, MSG
  9. Mix thoroughly and then add eggs and scallions
  10. Lastly give it a final toss and mix and you should be ready to serve!
